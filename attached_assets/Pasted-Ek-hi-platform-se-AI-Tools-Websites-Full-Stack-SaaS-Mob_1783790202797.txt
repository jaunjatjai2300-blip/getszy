Final Vision

Ek sentence me:

Getszy is an AI Business Operating System where you can build, sell, deploy, automate, and scale any digital or commerce product from a single platform.

⭐ Meri taraf se ek last architectural suggestion

Main "Projects" ko Getszy ka center banaunga. Har website, SaaS, AI tool, mobile app, dropshipping store ya internal tool ek project hoga. Us project ke andar hi builder, AI, commerce, deployment aur analytics milenge.

Isse tum future me 100 alag product types support kar paoge bina navigation ko tod-phod kiye. Ye architecture long-term maintainable, modular aur enterprise-grade rahega.
